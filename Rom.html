Lam aphrang :

Pol, 58 A.D. aningkan kejut malon si Korinth pen Rom asor anhan kido ketok. Halabangso ahut alangli ha Spein kedamji pu mathathip lo lapen alangli ha Rom ta malomso kevurponji pu aning hang (Rom. 15:22). Bonta alangli hadak le un-edetlo. Pol ha Rom kele ke 6l A.D. malom lo lapen Birta Keme kapachethang abang ason kali bonta phatek kejang abang ason si kele.

Baibel along Pol akido aphai angbong Romn asor aphan kelo akido si bi paphranglo, pima alangli kido an angbong ke labangso akido helo akedingnei lapen akenangsotnei. Ladak ketok alam ke ahok pu kalakhaponji lapen nmonit atum kejokji ke amethang akam pen kali, Arnam akanghon kethe lapen Jisu Khrist ahirjume pen si jokji pu alam lo.

23*Bonta non ke labangso alongri neli keklem nangji akam avedetlo lapen neli nanglitum along kevangji pu ketir aningkan do-olo.

24*La apot si neli ha Spein kedam ahut nanglitum along nang vur longji lapen malomso bak nanglitum pen dorapdet aphi hadak kapeleji aphan nanglitum ne rapdunji pu si neli ning thelo.

25*Non mamat ke neli Kangthir atum aphan kerap-i ason kepidamji aphan ha Jerusalem dam nangjilang.

26*Pima ha Jerusalem kedo aKangthir atum angbong keduk kelak atum aphan kerap-i ason Masedonia lapen Akaia asor ning kangsam pen kacherungtang do.

27*Alanglitum labangso akam keklemji aning hang lapen keklemvek nangji akam lo, pima alanglitum Honghari atum pen Arnam ahirjume longrappetlo, la apot si Honghari atum ta alanglitum aphan pirthe akhi pen kerap nangji a-ot do.

28 Lasi neli labangso akam petangdet aphi lapen kacherung kacherektang an ason alanglitum aphan pidamdet aphi nanglitum along nang vur si ha Spein adet dampo.

29*Neli nanglitum along kevang ahut Khrist ahirjurne nang vanpi-o thekji pu si neli chinilo.

30*Korte atum, ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist lapen Karjong akanghon pen ajok nel1 pen cherap ra Arnam angno ne ardompitha pu si neli nang choningrilo.

31*Lake neli Judia kedo akekroikre atum arideng pen kejok thekji lapen neli Jerusalem asor aphan keklempi akam Kangthir atum aning kadok thekji aphan lo. 32*Lasi Arnarn aning kehang ateng neli nanglitum along ning arong pen vang long si nanglitum pen sang longrap-rappo.

33*Ning kangsam kepi a-Arnam nanglitum kadokave pedonang. Amen.

Kardom kachepi :
1*Senkre-e amondoli aDikonpi, ilitum angjir Phebe aphan neli nanglitum arideng si nang hodailo.

2*Hemphu amahang lang ra Kangthir atum aphan kedeng a ot adur ateng le alangliphan dengtha lapen alangli kenangji ason an ta rapduntha, pima alangli ke penang an atum aphan lapen neliphan ta ne raplo.

3*Khrist Jisu amen pen neli pen kam keklem raprap Priska lapen Akuila atum aphan neli kardom pitha.

4*Neli pran ne kapejokji pu alanglitum kethi nangji anta chechaklo, neli chot kali Honghari atum amondoli an ta alanglitum aphan aning chingduklo.

5*Alanglitum ahem kachingrum amondoli aphan ta neli kardom pitha. Asia angbong Khrist aphan kekroicheng abang neli kachinghon Ephenetus aphan ta neli kardom pitha.

6*Nanglitum angbong kam keklem kachepeduk abang Meri aphan ta neli kardom pitha.

7*Neli khei amonit lapen neli long phatek nang kejangdun Andronikus lapen Junias atum aphan ta neli kardom pitha. Alanglitum ke ketoitang angbong akethetum lo lapen neli.Khrist aphan kekroikre ako pen ta kekroi paphrang atum lo.

8*Khrist pen ajok neli kachinghon abang Ampliatus aphan ta neli kardom pitha.

9*Khrist pen ajok nelitum along kam nang kacherapdun abang Urbanus lapen neli kachinghon abang Stakis aphan ta neli kardorn pitha.

10*Khrist amen pen kesung kelak kephonang-o abang Appelles aphan neli kardom pitha. Aristobulus ahem asangho atum aphan ta neli kardom pitha.

11*Neli khei amonit Herodion aphan ta neli kardom pitha. Hemphu aphan kekroitang Narsisus ahem asangho ahem asangho atum aphan ta neli kardom pitha. 12*Hemphu amen pen neli long kam nang kacherapdun Triphena lapen Triphosa atum aphan ta neli kardom pitha. Hemphu amen pen kam keklem kachepeduk abang neli kachinghon Persis aphan ta neli kardom pitha.

13*Khrist pen ajok anong athang kajume abang Ruphus lapen alangli pei ati atum aphan ta neli kardom pitha. Alangli ke neli pei ason thakthak lo.

14*Asinkritus, Phlegon, Hermis, Patrobas, Hermas lapen alanglitum along kedo akorte atum aphan an ta neli kardom pitha.

15*Philologus, Julia, Nereus lapen alangli ingjirpi lapen Olimpas lapen e alanglitum along kedo akKangthir atum an aphan ta neli kardom pitha.

16*Kar-u pangthir pen le kardom chepi seroinoi, Khrist amondoli an ta nanglitum aphan kardom nang chepidunlo.

Kapacheremek :

18*Pima lapuson amonit atum ke tum ke Khrist Hemphu aphan le kam keklempi kali bonta amthang apok si kachelanachot. Alanglitum alam a kedok-lem lapen kero parlin pen angthikdut chomosoidet.
