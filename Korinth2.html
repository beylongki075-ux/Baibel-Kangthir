KORINTH ASOR APHAN POL KETOK APHRANG AKIDO

Lam aphrang :

57 A.D. aningkan ningkreng vangringring ahut ha Masedonia pen si Pol labangso akido ketok. Pol Korinth asor aphan ketok aphrang akido lapen aphi akido adak Korinth amondoli angbong penang an aning ke-oi alam klanglo. Pol ke Jisu asangho binong kali lapen alangli ke kahingno abang pu Jihudi atum alangliphan putotlo. Pol, aning-oi parpik si (2 Kor. 2:1) malomso ha Korinth damlo, bonta bang atum alangli phan keputot kepusit apot alangli kedam pen ale avedet si vangjuilo (2 Kor. 2:5-11: 7:12). Halabangso ahut alangli Korinth asor aphan penang alam kangtang tok si lolo. Halabangso akido pen ale do-ilo. Mondoli angbong ason ason akahingno keklem atum aphan pesunglo (2 Kor. 2:6) lapen Pol ke sangho binong lo pu aman dolo (2 Kor. 2:12-13; 7:5-8).

Ladak athak ketok alam pen Pol Korinth asor aphan ketok akido pakni kali pakthom do; bonta angbong kelo akido abang ke virdetlo lapen non ilitum along kedo Korinth asor aphan ketok akido hini abang pu ke, Pol ketok akido kethom abang lo.

Labangso akido ke Pol aning arlo kedo alam bang aphan kapeklangdak alam lo. Alangli monit ason ke chepebihek, bonta Sangho inut ason ke cherokalo. Pol sangho aphan kachiniji aphan aning kehang abang ke labangso akido porhivek nangji.

10*Nanglitum a-ot adur nang kapahingnoji aphan kali, bonta nang kapemeji aphan si Hemphu neliphan ne kepi ajakong pen neli nanglitum along kevang ahut nanglitum aphan nang kapesung nangnedetji aphan si neli vangve ako laheihei alam nang ketok lo. 11*Korte atum, kejut akardom deng-iktha. Nanglitum alam akam an chepeme chepechoknon, neli nang kachoningri alam arjunon, ning isipet pen le donon, anke kanghon lapen ning kangsam a-Arnam nanglitum along dopo.

12*Kar-u pangthir pen le kardom chepi seroinon.

13*Kadokave aKangthir atum ta nanglitum aphan kardom nang chepidunlo.

14*1litum aHemphu Jisu Khrist ahirjume, Arnam akanghon lapen Karjong Kangthir pen kedorappet ajakong nanglitum kadokaved along pedonang.
