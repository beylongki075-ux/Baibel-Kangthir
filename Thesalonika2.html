THESALONIKA ASOR APHAN POL KETOK APHI AKIDO

Angdeng kethek
Ning kachingduk, kachepaningdingji aphan kachoningri 1:1-12.
Khrist kevangthuji arni alam alam 2:1-12.
Chepaningding nangji, keklem chepeduk nangji lapen chingkime nangji pu si kapere --2:13-3:28.
chingkime nangji pu si kapere --2:13-3:28.

Kardom kachepi :
1.
1*Pol, silvanus lapen Timothi,Po Arnam lapen Hemphu Jisu Khrist pen Kachelangpet Thesalonika amondoli asor aphan ketok akido.

Po Arnam alpen Hemphu Jisu Khrist ahirjume lapen aning kangsam nanglitum along pedonang.

Kapadusi akangsai :
3*Korte atum, nanglitum an sose kroithipbomlo lapen nanglitum angbong anuttin ta an sose chinghon seroibomlo, la apot si nanglitum pen apot nelitum kaike ta Arnam aphan ning kachingduk nangji akam dokoklo lapen lake keklem meme akam ta lo.

4*Nanglitum kesung kelak lapen kachenek kaholo pho-o seta laheihei chechak si krojthipbomlo lapen dothipbomlo pu nanglitum alamjir Arnam amondoli angbong nelitum chepindak longlo.

5*Laheihei ke nanglitum aphan ha Arnam arat kedodun longji a-ot nang kapedo ahut, Arnam ke ahok pen si kangsai abang lo pu peklangdakpo; nanglitum non kesung kelak nanglitum non kesung kelak kachechak ke Arnam arat kedodun longji aphan lo.

6*Arnam ahok akangsai ateng ke nanglitum aphan nang kapesung atum aphan alangli pesungvekpo.

7*Mo Hemphu Jisu pherangke kethe atum penpen ha sining pen kevang ahut alangli nanglitum, atheke non kesung kelak kachechakbom atum aphan lapen nelitum aphan ta ning ne pangsampo,

8*Halabangso arni ke Arnam aphan kekroimo atum aphan lapen ilitum aHemphu Jisu aBirta Keme kekroikre atum aphan kapesungji aphan alangli kekar-ang ame angbong pen si vangji.

9*Halabangso arni alanglitum ke kejut un-ce akesung phoji atheke alanglitum Hemphu angno lapen alangli jakong kethe adak atur angbong dodun longledetji.

10*Halabangso arni monit kangthir atum angbong Hemphu adak atur theji lapen kekroi atum alangli dak alangli tur aphan pasingnakji; nelitum nang kethan alam karju pen ajok nanglitum ke kekroi atum lo.

11*Arnam nanglitum aphan nang kehang ateng nanglitum aphan a-ot nang kapedoji aphan lapen alangli jakong nanglitum keme aning kehang lapen kekroi akam an kapeplengji aphan nelitum Arnam nang ardompiverlo.

12*Ilitum a-Arnam lapen aHemphu Jisu Khrist ahirjume pen nanglitum angbong ilitum aHemphu Jisu amen adak atur theji lapen alangli along nanglitum adak atur ta thedunji.

Jisu kevangthuji alam :
2
1&2*Bang nang kethanmek pen tame, bang nang kethan alam pen tame lapen nelitum nang ketok akido lo pu bang nang kelothap akido alam pen tame, Hemphu arni nang letanglo pu ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist kevangthuji arni lapen ilitum alangli ingno kachingrumpetji amjir bang nang than pachobei seta manghudunri lapen kamatha chepesungri.

3*Ajat abidi pen tam badu ta nanglitum aphan nang pachobei longri; pima halabangso arni nang lele ako Arnam aphan kekoi atum do-oji lapen aron kekroikre abang atheke kevirtang abang ta nang cheklangji.

4*Halabangso abang, monit atu arnam pu kepu atum an aphan lapen kangduk kardom ason an aphan dongthipji lapen amethang chepethe si arnam pu chepu si Arnam ahem cheraithipji.

5*Neli nanglitum along kedo ahut pen ta neli laheihei alam nang thanlo pu nanglitum ning ave na?

6*Mo halabangsoabanga nang kacheklangji arni nang leletik ke kopi si alangphan kekhang ma lake nanglitum ta chinilo.

7*Aron kekroikre abang ke non pen ta dojoijelo; bonta alangphan kekhang abang cheterek-retik ke alang dengrong ke klem haihe.

8* Mo halabangso abang cheterek lote aron nkekroikre abang nang cheklangpo lapen Hemphu Jisu alangphan but-lampo lapen alangli kevangthu adak atur pen alangphan pevirdetpo.

9*Hala aron kekroikre abang hi-i ajakong pen nang tet si ason ason ahok-he akamanghu meme akam lapen kaphere meme angthek heihei

10*Kevirdetji peklangpo. atum aphan kapamanghuji aphan ason ason abidi tevarji. Halatum kejok-iji ahok alam ke dengdedet lapen kanghon chepavedet apotvirdetji. 11*Alanglitum ahok alam kekroidundedet aphan Arnam alanglitum angbong kapamanghu ajakong lophotlo.

12*Lake ahok alam kroidundedetsi pap keklem aning kehang atum aphan adai peklangdaksi kapesung longji aphan lo.

Kachepaningdingji aphan aning kaperung :
13*Hemphu kachinghon akorte atum, Karjong Kangthir nang kapangthir pen lapen ahok alam kekroidun pen nanglitum kejok longji aphan si Arnam nanglitum aphan hako pen ta nang invailo, lasi nanglitum pen apot nelitum kaike ta Arnam aphan ning kachingduk nangji akam dokoklo.

14*La apot si nanglitum ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist adak atur kelongdunji aphan nelitum nang kethan aBirta Keme pen ajok Arnam nanglitum aphan nang hanglo.

15*Lasi, korte atum, alam pen tame, akido pen tame, nelitum nang kethan alam lapen aron heihei rimthip ra kedo chepethirnon.

16*Ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist mamat lapen ilitum aPo Arnam, atheke ilitum aphan e-inghonsi alangli hirjume pen kaike ning ekapepliver lapen ning ekapethever abang,

17*nanglitum aning nang pepli ra lam keme kam keme an nang papehirnang.

Kachoningri :
3.
1*Korte atum, nanglitum angbong asonthot nahok-hok ta Hemphu alam an sose kachethangji lapen anong ketheji aphan,

2*lapen nelitum dhorom kave lapen kahingno amonit atum arideng pen kejokji aphan nelitum aphan Arnam ne ardompitha; pima kadokave le kekroi kedopet kali.



3*Hemphu ke chingkimesen; alangli nanglitum aphan nang pethir si kahingno abang arideng pen nang raidunpo.

4*Nelitum hukum nang pi si nang kethan alam an nanglitum kroibomlo lapen mo mo ta kroibomji pu nelitum Hemphu along ning thelo.

5*Nanglitum Arnam aphan kanghonji lapen Khrist aphan kekroithipji aphan Arnam nanglitum aning nang pejaponnang.

Kaselet lapen kachingrire :
6*Korte atum, nelitum nang kethan alam lapen aron ateng kedode lapen kaselet akote pen chelangri pu ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist amen pen nelitum nang kepu pesodaklo.

7*Nanglitum, nelitum ason nang charlidun nangji pu ke nanglitum mamat ta chinilo, nelitum ke nanglitum along kedo ahut chepaseletle.

8*Nelitum ke nanglitum akhi adat nang chopi pejoije; nanglitum angbong badu Aphan ta nang kapesungsedetji aphan nelitum jo arni ta kam keklem chepeduklo.

9*Lake nelitum, nanglitum akhi adat nang kechodunji aron ave pu aphan kali, honta nanglitum ta nelitunm ason nang kacharlidun thekji aphan si nang kapeklang lo.

10*Kaseet abang ke pecho longlenang pu nelitum annglitum along kedo ahut ta anng kepu pesodaklo.

11*nanglitum angbong penang an atum ke selet-o-o,papengpong chot,thangnat ta klemkle pu si nelitum arjulong.

12*Lapuson amonit atum papengpongpe ra amethang kecho longji aphan kam pachoklemnang pu si nelitum Hemphu Jisu Khrist amen pen alnglitum aphan ning perunglo lapen kepu pesodaklo.

Korte atum,kam keme keklemji aphan chepa-ainiri.

14*Komane jongsi nelitum labangso akido along ketok alam kroima lote alangli phan kapatherakji aphan alangli pen chelangri;

15*Bonta alangliphan hotur aphan ason le pere phelengnon.

Kerip alam :
16*Ning kangsam aHemphu nanglitum aphan akam aling along.tame akike ning kangsam nang pepinang.Hemphu nanglitum kadokave along pedonang.

17*Neli,Pol, neli methang ari pen si labangso akardom kechepi alam toklo.Neli, apaktin akido along ta angthek la helo; neli ketok aron lapu helo.

18*Iliyum aHemphu Jisu Khrist ahirjume nanglitum kadokave along pedonang.
