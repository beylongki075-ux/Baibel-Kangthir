PITOR KETOK APHRANG AKIDO

Lam aphrang
64 A.D. aningkan malom Rom pen si Pitor labangso akido Kepratsei aJihudi atum angbong pen Khristian keduntang atum ephan ketok. (1:1). Labangso akido along ketok athepi alam ke kesung kelak kepho ahut kachechak kachepaningding nangi lapen baptisma kelong pen keklem nangji akam heihei si ketok.

Angdeng kethak
1.Baptisma pen mahang kethekthu apot ning kachingduk (1:1-12).
2.Baptisma pen monit kangthir-i (1:13-2:10).
3.A-ot keme, kachingkime, Khristian ahem arit (2:11- 3:12).
4.Kesung kelak kepho ahut Jisu ason kachechak (3:13-4:19).
5.Mondoli asarthe atum akam; kardom kachepi (5:1-14).

Kardom kachepi :
1.
1*Jisu Khrist aketoitang abang Pitor,ha Pontus, Galasia,Kapadosia,Asia lapen Bithinia ahavar bithe bitha kepratsei aphan ketok akido aKhristian atum aphan ketok akido.

2*Nanglitum Jisu Khrist anban kroi si alangli vi nang karsi longji aphan Po Arnam nanglitum aphan nang ingvai si Karjong Kangthir pen nang pangthirtanglo. Nanglitum along hirjume lapen ning kangsam pedonang.

Nanglitum kekroitang apot ha kejut arni kapeklangji aphan kesiktang akejok kelongji aphan Arnam alangli jakong pen nanglitum aphan nang pedo pemelo.

Laheihei alam Arnam apherangke atum ta chini ingtung-o.

13*La apot si nanglitum aning chepethirthip pen no kang-ar pen le do ra mo Jisu Khrist nang kacheklangji arni nanglitum kelongdunji ahirjume aphan le ning chetheklongnon.

14*Nanglitum ahok alam kachinine ako ke nanglitum aning kehang aling keklemthap asonthot ke klemri.

15*Bonta Arnam aphan kroi ra nanglitum aphan nang kehang abang le kangthir asonthot nanglitum ta keklem aling ta chepangthirsetnon.

16*Pima "Ne ke ingthirset apot nangtum chepangthirsetnon" pu Ketok Kangthir along toktanglo.

18*Nanglitum aphusar atum along pen kerimdun kahingno akam ajam pen nanglitum aphan nang kapejok ke kevirdetji aser arup pen kali;

21*Jisu pen ajok si nanglitum Amam aphan kroi longlo. Nanglitum Arnam aphan kekroi lapen aning kachethe longji aphan si alangli Jisu aphan kethi atum angbong pen nang perengthu si adak atur pethelo.

Kanghon alam :
22*Nanglitum ahok alam kekroi pen nanglitum tebok terei atum aphan kanghon peme thekji aphan si nanglitum akarjong pangthirtanglo, lasi nanglitumn ning aklong pen chinghon seroinon. Kevirdetji achili pen le nanglitum nang keklang kali, kevir un-epin achili pen si nang keklang, atheke kerengver lapen kedover Arnam alam pen si nanglitum amahang thekthulo.

24*Pima Monit ke bap ason thakthak lapen alangli dak alangli tur an ta bap angphar ason thakthak. Bap ke chekedudet lapen angphar ta ingiuidet.

25*Bonta Hemphu alam ke kaike ta dover." (a) pu Ketok Kangthir along ketok do. Halabangso alam ke nanglitum aphan nang kethan aBirta Keme helo.

1*Apiso atum, nanglitum apenan-nan atum aphan kroi ra aman apeng pitu, anke penang an atu atum ke Arnam alam le kroikre seta lam ethe pupepin pen ta apiso atum a-ot adur keme pen ajok alanglitum aphan pekroi thekpo.

5*Hako ahut Arnam aphan aning kachethe a-arlosomar kangthir atum ta lapuson si matlep-laplo.

7*Apenan atum, nanglitum ta lapuson thakthak kereset pen le nanglitum apiso atum pen chedorapnon, pima arlosomar atum ke ajakong doparpe. 4*Non ke nanglitum, alanglitum along halaheihei a-ot aji kave akam keklemdundedet apot alanglitum manghuphronglo lapen nanglitum aphan nang pinremlo.

Mondoli asarthe atum aphan kapere :
5.
1*Neli ke Khrist akesung kelak kachechak ahaki lo lapen mo keklangji adak atur kelongdunji abang lo. Neli mondoli asarthe atum angbong inut,nanglitum angbong aSarthe atum aphan choningrilo.

2*Nanglitum aphan nang kahodai Arnam ameresak ahur aphan langnon, nanglitum aning ar-i ar-a pen kali, ning kehang peme pen; katherak meme ale keri pen kali, keklem kangtung peme pen;

Khrist arat atum aphan kapere :
5*Lason thakthak nanglitum riso nimso atum ta mondoli aSarthe atum aphan kroitu. Nanglitum kadokave ta chepebihek-hak pen le dotu, pima "Arnam ke kacheroka atum aphan aning dokde, bonta kachepebihek atum aphan ke hirjume pi."

6*Arnam ajakong kethe arum le chepebihek pen donon, anke apor nang kele ahut alangli nanglitum aphan nang pethepo.

7*Alangli ke nanglitum aphan nang langver, lasi nanglitum aning kebi ason an ta alangliphan le hodainon.

8*Ahokpet pen lapen no kang-ar pen le donon. Nanglitum ahotur ke kangrongbai ahingho ason, komane hi-i aphan cho longji mene pu si chorivunglo.

9*Pirthe adang kedo akorte atum ta nanglitum asonthot kesung kelak pho nanglo pu chini ra kekroi kethirthip pen le alangphan phlungnon.

10*Nanglitum malomso kesung phodet aphi hirjume an kepi a-Arnam, nanglitum aphan Jisu Khrist pen ajok kejut un-e adak atur kelongji aphan nang kehang abang mamat si nanglitum aphan nang pemethupo, nang pedothippo lapen nang pethirthippo.

11*Alangliphan kaike ta jakong horang pedover nang. Amen.
