KETOITANG ATUM AKAM AKITAP

Lam aphrang
Ketoitang atum akam akitap ketok abang ke Luk helo pu si Mondoli asor kitap ketok atum an ta pupet. Luk ke Siria adet Antiok asor. Alangli ke se kelang abang lapen Honghari pen Khristian keman abang (Kol. 4:10-14). Pol sangho Birta Keme kapachethangdam ahut batai hini aphong lapen batai kethom aphong alangli Pol along dun. Pol aphan nep si alangliphan phongni ha Rom kepon ahut ta Luk alangli long dodun (Kol. 4:14; Phile. I:24; 2 Tim. 4:11).

Birta Keme akitap kethom abang lapen Ketoitang atum akam akitap ta Luk si kelo (Lk. 1:1; Akam. 1:1).

Birta Keme akitap along Jisu Khrist akai angbong alam akam an ta tokphlirlo (Lk. 1:3). Akam akitap along Jisu Khrist keseng tekang aMondoli alamjir kecheng pen pangcheng si mondoli kechan lapen kethir alam si ketok. Labangso akitap along ketok athepi alam ke "Nanglitum ha Jerusalem lapen ha Judia lapen Samaria lapen pirthe kejut an ta neli haki cheplang nangji" pu Jisu kethan alam lo (Akam. 1:8). Labangso akitap arlo ketok alam kecheng ke ha Jerusalem Jihudi atum angbong mondoli kechan alam pen lo lapen aphi ke ha Rom Honghari atum angbong mondoli kechan alam lo. Rom arongsopi ke pirthe angbongchit ason lo. hadak Birta Keme kele pen Luk ketok alam ta pejutlo.

Akam akitap along ketok alam ke Jisu ha sining kedamthu arni pen lake 30 A.D aningkan pen pangcheng si Pol aphan kenep pen ha Rom kepon arni an, atheke 63 A.D. aningkan angbong alam lo.

Labangso akitap along angdengpi I pen ha 15 an ke Pitor akam lapen Jerusalem, Judia lapen Samaria amondoli alamjir lo. Khristian adhorom ke Jihudi atum aphan chot ma kalite kadokave amonit atum aphan ta ma pu alam labangso ahut mondoli arlo klanglo. Khristian adhorom ke Jihudi atum adohrom ma? Kalite Katholik atheke pirthe adang adhorom ma? Honghari atum Khristian nang dunde ako Jihudi cheplang nangji ma? Kejok ke Moses along pen tong nang keklang ma, kalite Khrist along pen si nang keklang

ma? Kornelius pu abang inut allonghari amonit aphan Pitor ALOT baptisma kepi pen (Akam. 10-11: 18) lapen akaprek atum Khristian nang kedunji aning kehang atum ke Jihudi cheplang paphrang nangne lo pu ha Jerusalem kangni Sangho atum ameipi along kaperai pen ajok (Akam. 15: 1-35) Khristian adhorom Jihudi adhorom pen chekak si Katholik binong cheplanglo.

Aphi-phi angdengpi an ke Tarsus asor Pol pu inut amonit akam alamjir lo. Karjong Kangthir kejapon pen ajok Pol aning cheruidet aphi alangli 'Honghari atum aSangho' cheplanglo (Rom. 11:13). Pol batai kethom aphong Birta Keme kapachethangdam ahut lapen batai hini aphong alangliphan nep si phatek kebengbe ako alangli Asia Minor, Masedonia, Gris lapen Rom bithe bitha ta Birta Keme pachethanglo.

70-90 A.D. aningkan malom si Luk ha Rom arongsopi Grik alam pen si Ketoitang atum akam akitap ketok.
