KORINTH ASOR APHAN POL KETOK APHRANG AKIDO

Lam aphrang :

Batai kethom aphong Pol Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedam ahut 57 A.D. aningkan Barla vangbarbar ahut si Pol labangso akido ha Ephesus pen si ketok. Batai hini aphong Pol Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedam ahut 50 A.D. aningkan malom si alangli Korinth amondoli keseng. Korinth ke Akaia angbong isi arongsopi lo. Hadak tangka maha kapelong akam aphan pethepik, hadak mek-langno no-langno akam ta do-o lapen Honghari adhorom ta thIrthip. Pol hadak ningkan isi ra ebeng anbak kedo ahut Jihudi atum ke alangli lam kroidunvangve, bonta Honghari atum ke alangli lam kekroi atum do-olo (Akam. 18:1-8; 18:11). Pol damdet aphi ke Apollos si hadak kedo lapen mondoli akam keklem.

57 A.D. aningkan Pol ha Ephesus kedo ahut (1 Kor. 16:8; Akam. 19:1-20) Korinth amondoli abirta hingno pu arjulonglo. Mondoli angbong abe kachevung do, kahingno amonit atum kekroitang atum pen kedo chejusai, Eukharist akam ahut ta ason ason akahingno do, Karjong Kangthir ahirjume panahokdet lapen monit nang kerengthu alam lapen kache-en kachedo akam angbong ta ason ason kamatha kechokche do pu arjulonglo. Laheihei alam kapekengji pu si Pol labangso akido ketok.
