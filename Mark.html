MARK KETOK ABIRTA KEME

Lam aphrang :
Mark ketok aBirta Keme' pu aBirta keme ketok abang ke Meri asopo John Mark pu abang helo. Alangli ke Jerusalem si kedo (Akam. 12:12)

lapen Barnabas ajirpo lo (Kol. 4:10). Pol lapen Barnabas Birta Keme kapachethangdamcheng ahut John Mark ta alanglitum along do (Akam. 12:25; 13:5). Aphi ke alangli Pol pen chekak si (Akam 13:13; 15:37) Barnabas along si kachelangdun (Akam. 15:39).

Aphi ke Pol ha Rom kedo ahut alangli Pol along cherapdun lo (Phil. 24; 2 Tim. 4:11; Kol. 4:1O). John Mark aphan kekroi angbong kapethir abang ke Pitor helo. Pitor alangliphan 'Neli sopo Mark' pu si kehang (1 Pit. 5:15).

Mondoli angbong angthun kevangjir alam ateng John Mark abang penang an aningkan Pitor along kam cherapdunlo. Getsemani abiri arlo Jisu aphan keneppon ahut kangdukni ape epak chepindeng si Jisu aphi kedun abang lapen sipahi atum alangliphan kenep bor-i ahut ape chehektekangjok si areng angse kekat arisomar ke John Mark helo tahai (Mk. 14:51). Aphi ke John Mark ke Aleksandria aBishop manlo pu kepu atum ta do, bonta labangso alam ke ahaki ave.

'Mark ketok aBirta Keme' pu akitap ke John Mark si ketok. Alangli honghari pen Khristian keduntang atum aphan si labangso aBirta Keme Grik alam pen ha Rom si ketok pu si pupet. (65 A.D. anigkan malom si labangso akitap ketok).
Kadokave amonit atum aphan jasemet ke honghari atum aphan Birta Keme kapachethang thekji aphan si Mark labangso aBirta Keme ketok. Arnam arat nang teboktanglo, nanglitum ning cherui ra Birta Keme kroihurnon' (Mk. 1:15).

Jisu Khrist ke Arnam binong lapen monit binong lo pu kapeklangji aphan si Mark ke kamatha. Alangli ketok alam lapuson si kapangcheng: "Arnam aSo Jisu Khrist aBirta Keme kecheng'" (ME. 1:1) Po Arnam amethang mamat si Jisu ke alangi Sopo lo pu si ha sining pen haki kechodun (Mk. l:11; 9:7)

Alangli ke Arnam ajakong do pu haki nang chodun, alangli pap kadia ajakong do (Mk. 2:10-12); alangli ke kesang arni ahemphu lo (Mk. 2: 28; 3:1-5), alangli ke hi-i kephlung ajakong do (Mk. 1:28-34; 3:11). Alangli ke kapatu alam kachini ajakong do (Mk. 2:8; 8:17; 12:15). Alangli ke kethanmek ajakong do (Mk. 8:31; 10:19; 13:1).

Alangli ke Arnam aSo' pu kapachiniji aphan Jisu adak atur kachepeklang abatai do-o, atheke alangli Birta Keme kapachethangcheng ahut (Mk. 1:), alangli arje kachepharlo ahut (Mk. 9:7), Kalvari anglong athak alangli kethi ahut sipahi kethe abang kapeklang alam (Mk. 15:39).

Bonta Jisu ke Arnam aSo binong lo seta monit binong ta lo. Jisu ke monit binong lo pu anut kaprek alam ketok aBirta Keme along apar te Mark ketok aBirta Keme along klangdakmu. Jisu telong arlo amek kejang (Mk. 4:37) - Alangli osomar atum aphan kanghon (Mk. 9:36; 10:16) - Alangli ke baroi asopo (Mk. 6:3) Alangli ke monit atum asonthot si kedo (Mk. 3:5; 6:34; 8:2; 10:14, 16, 21; 14:41).

Aphi an ke alangli kesung kelak kachechak ahut alangli ke monit binong lo pu klangdaklo. Alangli helo kesung kelak kachechak aMessia, alangli jirpo atum alangliphan chini pememedetlo, alangli hotur atum alangliphan chedanlo, alangli methang arat ta alangliphan chini pu pupedetlo (Mk. 5:40; 6:2; 2:1-3:6; 4:13).
