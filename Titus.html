TITUS APHAN POL KETOK KIDO

LAm aphrang
Titus ke Pol ari sangho lapen ajirpo lo.Alangli men ke Ketoitang atum akam akitap along klangkle lapen alamjir chiniparpe.Pol along alngli ta ha Jerusalem dun (Gal. 2:1). Jihudi aKhristian atum alangliphan a-ok kangduk areng angchun pacherottoilo,bonta Pol ke krodunde (Gal 2:3). KOrinth asor kachebohongsai alam kapapekengdamji aphan Pol alangliphan ha Korinth toilo (2 Kor. 7:13-15; 8:16-24). Along along amondoli kelangdam padu ahut (64-66 aningkan angbong) Pol,Titus aphan Kret amondoli aphan pacharitoilo.(Tit.1:5). 64-65 A.D. aningkan malom si Titus aphan Pol,Masedonia pen si labangso akido ketok.Labangso akido ke Timothi aphan aphrang kelo akido ketok ahut malom si ketok lpaen ketok athe ta isipet.

Angdeng kethak
1. Titus akam: Mondoli asarthe atum aphan kangvai alam,kaphola alam kethan atum pen kachedan (1:1-16).

2. MOndoli keja aron: Khristian a-ot adur; arta kedo atum aphan lapen Mondoli keja atum aphan aman kepi; chethe kimi;kechokche alam kahili (2:1-3:11). 3. Kardom kachepi (3:12-15).

Mondoli asarthe atum aphan kebat :
5*Ason ason alam akam kaperaiji lapen neli nang kapinkhat ateng arong arong mondoli asarthe atum aphan kebatji aphan si neli nangliphan ha Kret nang pedolo.

6*Alanglitum ke bang kedin move atum nangji, apiso ingdeng isivet atum nangji, alanglitum asomar atum ta kekroitang atum, kachingkimesen lapen bang kepu bang kepu move atum nangji.

7*Pima mondoli asarthe ke Arnam akam keklem abang pinak apot bang din movedet nangji; alangli ke ane kachenethe kalite kehaise abang, kalite kangri kangkrang abang, kalite kasedeng abang kalite tangka maha amek kethe abang kalidet nangji.

8*Alangli ke aning keme, akeme aning kehang, bang aphan kamatha thek, aning kekeng, kangthirset lapen aning kacheber thek abang nangji.

9*Ahok alam kethan thekji lapen alangliphan kachesong atum aphan kaperem thekji aphan alangli ke akrok alam an ke-othip abang nangji.

Kethan pachobei atum aphan kachesong :

11*Alanglitum ke bang aphan ason ason alam kethan ajakong ave, bonta tangka maha amek kethe pen ajok thanthap thanphru si alanglitum atum kethan alam pen adon adon amonit aphan ta pamanghu pamangramlo, lasi alanglitum aphan pedoklip nangji.

13*Labangso alam ke ahok.

14*Lasi alanglitum aphan kekroi angbong kapedo pemeji lapen Jihudi atum kethanthap alam, kalite ahok alam kevar atum ahukum kapekroidundedetji aphan alanglitum aphan tam masi.

15*Kangthirset amonit atum aphan ke kadokave ta akangthir angse lo, bonta kahingno lapen kekroikre atum aphan ke thangnat ta kangthir ason ave; alanglitum aning angthin lapen kamatha si kahingno.

16*Arnam aphan chini pu si alanglitum kachekrong, bonta alanglitum akam pen ke alanglitum Amam aphan kachini angthek ave; alanglitum ke hijim meme, chingkino-o-o lapen thangnat akam keme keklemji ta a-ot ave.

2*Sarmar atum ke ingjir ingkrangkredet nangji, bang aphan matha theser nangji,aning cheber thek nangji,kekroi angbong kedo thirthip nangji,kekroi angbong keddo thirthip nangji,kanghon do nangji lapen aning chepeding thek nangji pu alnglitum aphan thannon.
