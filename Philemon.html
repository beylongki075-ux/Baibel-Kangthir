PHILEMON APHAN POL KETOK AKIDO

Lam aPhrang
Labangso akido ke Pol amethang si ketok pu ke kadokave ta kroipet. Labangso akido ke Pol, 62 A.D. aningkan malom si ha Rom phatek kedo ahut si ketok. Philemon ke Kolose asor lo, alangli ke plang-o lapen ha Ephesus si Pol alangli phan Baptisma kepi, Onesimus, Philemon aban, alangli hemphu along pen katjui si ha Rom lelo lapen hadak Pol arideng si alangli baptisma denglo. Onesimus aphan Pol ha ahemphu along toithulo lapen alangliphan dengthutha pu si Philemon aphan Pol choningrilo.

Labangso akido along eson akenangsot alam okok apot si ilitum labangso akido rimbomlo. Honghari atum angbong ke ban sot kachebi do-o seta Onesimus aphan ke "ban ason kali bonseta korte ason" (Phil l:6) si Philemon aphan Pol papedengthutoilo. Lason alam ke Khristian adhorom ansi thanchot:

Angdeng kethak
1. Kardom kachepi (1:1-3).
2. Philemon akanghon lapen akekroi apot ning kachingduk (1:4-7)
3.Philemon, Onesimus aphan korte chekam si dengthu nangji pu Pol kachoningri (1:8-21).
4.Kerip alam (1:22-25).

Onesimus aphan kachoningripi :
8*Nangli keklem nangji akam neli nang puthip un,

9*bonta neli nangliphan nang kanghon do, lasi neli nang choningrilo neli atheke Jisu pen jakong kelongtang abang lapen Jisu Khrist amen pen non phatek kedo abang Pol,

10*Neli sopo Onesimus aphan nang choningripilo,neli phatek kedo ahut si neli alangli po (a) cheplanglo.

11*(Aphrang ke nangliphan alangli thangnat ta ningjoino,bonta non ke nangliphan lapen neliphan ta ningjoime lo)

12*Neli non alangliphan nangli long nang toithulo, alangli ke neli kachinghonnei abang lo.

13*Birta. Keme pen, ajok non neli phatek, kedo ahut nangli men pen neliphan. ne kapelangji pu alangliphan neli long chebipon ingtung-olo.

14*Bonta nangli lamthe longle te neli thangnat ta klem ingtungte, pima lake nangliphan nang kapeklem bor-i akam kalidet nangji, bonta nangli ning kehang binong pen akam nangji.

15*Nangli alangliphan kebithuver longji aphan si alangli malomso ako nangli long pen kedam chot tahai.

16*Non ke ban kedam ateng kali lo,ban apar akethe lo, neliphan ke alngli neli kachinghonnei akorte inut lo lapen nangliphan ta ok anatthu tame Hemphu angn0 tame nangli kachinghonpar akorte inut lo.

17*Lasi neliphan nangli pen kam keklemrappet abang pu le ne matha lote neliphan asonthot le alangliphan ta dengtha.

18*Alangli jongsi nangli ingno kopine kahingno ason kachomatha do lepute, kalite nangliphan nang kevoithu nangji ason do lepute neli archakpo:

19*Neli atheke Pol neli methang ari pen si labangso alam ketok, neli si nang tupo nangli methang mamat neliphan ne kachepidet nangji alam do seta lake neli pudamdelo.

20*Neli korte, Hemphu angno nangli neliphan ne raptha. Hemphu angno neliphan ning arong ne pedotha.

Kerip alam :
21*Neli nang kachoningri alam pen apar ta nangli klemji pu neli chinilo, lasi nangli neli lam ne kroiji pu ning theklong pen neli laso akido nang ketok.

22*Non ethe alam dolang, nangli ne kardompi pen ajok neli nangli long kevang longji aphan ning kethe do, lasi neli phan kedo adim ne thikpitha.

23*Jisu Khrist amen pen neli pen phatek kedorap-rap abang Epaphras ta nangliphan kardom nang chepidunlo.

24*Lason thakthak neli pen kam keklem rap rap atum atheke Mark, Aristarkus, Demas lapen Luk atum ta kardom nang chepidunlo.

25*Hemphu Jisu Khrist ahirjume nangli karjong along pedonang.
