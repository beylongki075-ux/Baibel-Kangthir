Laheihei akitap ketok ke kethekser ser atum ketok aGrik alam pen le ketok kali, bonta Rom adetpi angbong Nihang anat amonit atum kaningje aGrik alam pen si ketok. Labangso aGrik alam ke kadokave amonit atum ta thekpet apot labangso aGrik alam pen si Jisu Khrist aBirta Keme pachethang-ilo.

Laheihei akitap ajat ajat do-o, ketok aum ta do-o, ketok athe ta do-o, seta laheihei akitap along ketok athepi alam ke isivet lapen kekroi ta isivet. Kopima ketok atum an ta asong isivet atheke Khrist keseng amondoli asor lo. Mondoli asor si mondoli asor aphan laheihei akitap ketok. Halabangso ahut ketoktang aBirta Kemne akitap ake-ongpi, kido heihei lapen kapeklang akitap ake-ongpi angbong pen kolo abang si Arnam akarjong ajakong pen ketok ma lake mondoli si perailo, pima laheihei akitap ke halabangso ahut kedo aKhristian atum aphan chot kali bonta pirthe ruretik kedoji amonit atum aphan si ketokpi lo. Laheihei akitap kaporhi atum kachini kejoi muchotji aphan laheihei akitap ketok apor ateng dak aber peklanglo.

Lam aphrang
Gospel

'Gospel' pu alamthe ke'Good Spell" pu Englishalamban pen si kevang.Labangso alam athe ke 'BirtaKeme' pu. Labangso alamtheGrik alam pen ke 'EVANJELION' pu si kepu, atheke Birta Keme.Rom arecho kethe Sesar asoamahang kethek alam lapen Sesar recho kecho alam kapachethang ahut 'Evanjelion'pu alamthe si ke-en.

Recho apar aRecho kethe lapen Kapejok abang Jisu amahang kethek alam (Lk. 2:10-11), alangli rat alangli det ke-ongji alam (Mt. 4:17; Mk. 1:15) lapen monit aso an aphan kapejok abang (Mk. 16:15) kevang alam kapachethang ahut 'Gospel' pu alamthe si ke-en.Kethanmek abang Isaia sipu alam kaningjecheng (Is. 40:9; 41:27;52:7). Halabangso akethanmek alam 'Gospel' pu alamjir ke Jisu along si pachekok-lo pu Jisu amethang mamat si Nasareth akardom ahem arlo lam kethan ahut thanlo (Is 61:1; Lk. 4:18).

Birta Keme ke kopi lo?

Epak akitap aphan le BirtaKeme pu kepudam kali. Birta keme ke Jisu Khrist kapachethang alam aphan si kepudam (Mt. 11:5; Akam. 5:42). Mondoli kecheng ahut ke kadokave aKhristian atum aphan ta 'Evanjelist' pusi kehang, pima kadokave ta Birta Keme kapachethang atum lo (Akam.21:8; Eph. 4:11; Rom. 1:1-21 Tim. 41:5).

Kecheng pen ta mondoli ilitum aphan ekepi lapen a-ove ove angthun kevangjir Birta Keme alam ketok akitap pak phli do. Lake Mathiu ketok aBirta Keme, Mark ketok aBirta Keme, Luk ketok aBirta Keme lapen John ketok aBirta Keme heihei helo. Birta Keme ke isivet, pima Mathiu aBirta Keme, Mark aBirta Keme lapuson ason ason aBirta Keme ke ave; Mathiu, Mark, Luk lapen John atum ketok aBirta Keme-Birta Keme ke esonvet lake Jisu Khrist aBirta Keme lo. Tangte hala bang phli amonit atum ketok aBirta Keme ke kopi lo? Laheihei ke ovelir akitap kali, Birta Keme ketok atum ke ovelir alam le ketok kali, lasi Jisu amahang kethek lapen kethi arni aniphai ta toktedetlo. Tangte laheihei ke Jisu akai isi alam ketok akitap ma? La ta kali, pima Mathiu ketok alam angbong akaprek atum ketokte ta do, John ketok alam angbong akaprek atum ketokte ta do.

Laheihei akitap ke dhorom angbong kachehili alam le ketok kali, pima laheihei akitap along Jisu kethan alam ke pethihek pen si ketok lapen Sangho atum kechokche alam ta ketok do. Dhorom angbong kachehili alam le ketok asonte laheihei alam ke toktedet nangji apot lo. Laheihei akitap pak phli ke Theoloji (Arnam alamjir) tong ketok ma? Kali, pima Birta Keme ketok atum ke kethan alam aphan ke lapu an mathathe, bonta kethan aBang atheke Jisu Khrist, kerengver abang, Arnam binong lapen monit binong aphan si kamatha lapen alangli lamjir si ketok.

Tangte Birta Keme pu ke kopi lo? Laheihei akitap arlo ketok alam ke mondoli kecheng ahut kapachethang alam aphan haki kechodun alam lo, atheke Jisu akam, alangli kethi, alangli nang kerengthu lapen alangli phan anong kapethe alam ahaki lo. Laheihei akitap ke kecheng ahut Khristian atum adhorom akitap lo. Laheihei alam ke Sangho atum aphuthak keseng amondoli Jisu Khrist pirthe kapejok akam ahaki kechodun helo. KACHETONPET ABIRTA KEME (SYNOPTIC GOSPEL) Bang phli ketok aBirta Keme le langdam ketok alam pen prekjam. Aphrangsi bang kethom atum ketok aBirta Keme aphan Synoptic Gospel' pulo. 'Synoptic' pu alamthe ke "Theklongpet' pu aphan si kepudam, pima la bang kethom ketok alam le biren lote langrappet me. Dak aber ketok alam langtha:

1.Jisu tomon thepi kapeham Mt. 8:23-27; Mk. 4:35-41; Lk. 8: 22-25. 2.Chili kerik abang alamlir Mt. 13:1-9; Mk. 4:1-9: Lk. 8:4-8.

3.Jisu arje kachepharlo-Mi. 17:1-9; Mk. 9:2-10: Lk. 9:28-36.

4.Sesar aphan medu kep! alam- Mt. 22:J5-22: MK. 12:13-17; Lk. 20:20-26.

5.Lapuson akaprek kapre angdeng do-olang.

La bang kethom atum ketok alam ke chingbarkre apot inut abang anut kaprek ketok alam pen si ke-en ma kalite la bang kethom atum ta eson alam nen si ke-en ma pu ilitum matha tahai. La bang kethom atum ketok alam ta chingbarkre, bonta alanglitum ketok alam angbong along pen along ke akeprek do, la helo ilitum kamanghudam adim.

Hemphu akardom Mathiu ketok lapen Luk ketok alam akeprek do (Mt. 6:9-13; Lk. 11:2-4).

Eukharist kebi alam ta akeprek do (Mi. 26:26-28: Mk. 14:22-24Lk. 22:19-20).

Jisu amahang kethek alam ketok ta prek-o. Lapuson alanglitum ketok alam angbong keprek adim do-o. Tangte alanglitum kopuson si ketok ma? Labangso karju alam kethakdun atum do-o, bonta Baibel kethekser-ser atum kepupet alam ke lapu helo:

Jisu ha sining chedamthudet aphi mondoli arlo Sangho atum kapachethang alam lapen halabangso aphi angthun angthun kachethanbom alam do. Jihudi atum aphan lapen Honghari atum aphan Jisu Khrist akai alam lapen alangli kethan alam kapachethang aron do; kardom kardi aron lapen Jisu aphan kachoningri aron lapen Jisu akai alam kaporhi ta do. 45 pen 55 A.D. aningkan angbong Jisu kethan alam kapangrum alam (Logia) lapen Aranmailk alam Pen Mathiu ketok do: keme pu pakni akitap ta klanglo. (Papias lapen akaprek amondoli aSarthe atum ta labangso akitap heihei kedo alam haki chodunlo). La pakni akitap ta Grik alam pen tokthuphitlo.

Halabangso akitap lapen Pitor kapachethang alam ensi heihei pen aningkan malom Mark, 65 A.D. alam pen Mark ketok aBirta keme' pu akitap tok-lo. Ningkan kep anbak aphi Luk lapen Mathiu (kalite Mathiu asangho inut) ketok alam anut kaprek chinine pin pen, Mark ketok aBirta Keme, Jisu kethan alam kapangrumtang akitap, Mathiu, Aramaik alam pen ketok aBirta Keme, laheihei alam pen en si amethang methang kachini ateng Luk ketok aBirta keme' lapen 'Mathiu ketok aBirta keme' pu akitap heihei tok-lo. Labangso pen kopu si la bang kethom ketok ketok kachingbarkre ma alam lake alanglitum kadokave ke-endam alam atiri isivet, lapen kopu alanglitum ketok alam keprek lake alanglitum amethang methang kachini ateng Si ketok pinak apot lo, pu ilitum chini.
