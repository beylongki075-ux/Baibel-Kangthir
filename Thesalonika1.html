THESALONIKI ASOR APHAN POL KETOK APHRANG AKIDO

Lam aphrang
Thesalonika ke Masedonia ahavar angbong isi arongsopi lo. 51 A.D. aningkan arjon angbong, Pol batai hini aphong Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedam ahut si Pol Thesalonika amondoli keseng (Akam. 17:1-10). 52 A.D. aningkan malom Pol ha Korinth laheihei akido pakni ta ketok. La pakni akido helo akimi aron angbong ketokcheng alam.

Laheihei akido ketok athe lapu helo: Kethitang atum mo pirthe kejut arni Jisu Khrist kevangthuji ahut adak aur longdundelo pu si alanglitum aning oruilo. Pol alanglitum aphan aning kapangsamji aphan laheihei akido tok si pulo: "Ahok amonit atum ke thilot seta akarjong ke aning arongver lapen Khrist kevangthuji arni ke kadokave ta nang rengthupo."

Laheihei alam pen chot ke Thesalonika asor ke aning ingsamsedet si Khrist kevangthuji arni nang tebok-hetlo tahai pu si alanglitum aning oiruibomlo. Lasi Pol batai hini aphong alanglitum aphan kido toklo. Ketok alam lapu helo: Khrist kevangthuji arni ke ajo kanghu kevang ason si cheplangji, lasi ilitum kachesik-et pen si do nangji

2*Nelitum Arnam kardom ahut nanglitum aphan nang parnoponver si Arnam aphan ning nang chingdukpilo.

3*Nanglitum ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist pen chelangpet si kekroi pen kam klemlo, kanghon pen klem chepeduklo lapen ning kethe pen klem pemeverlo pu alam ilitum a Arnam lapen aPo angno nelitum nang mathaverlo.

4*Arnam kachinghon nelitum akorte atum, nanglitum aphan alangli si nang kangvai pu nelitumn chini.

5*Pima nanglitum aphan, alam pen chot kali, bonta Arnam ajakong, Karjong Kangthir akerap lapen kekroithip pen si Birta Keme nang thanlo. Nanglitum kacharlidun longji aphan nanglitum angbong nelitum kopuson aron pen si kedo ma lake nanglitum ne chinilo.

6*Hemphu lapen nelitum asonthot si nanglitum ta nang charlidunlo. Nanglitum bang arideng kesung kelak le phonang-o seta Karjong Kangtkir pen kelong aning arong pen si nanglitum Birta Keme aphan kroilo.

7*Lasi Masedonia lapen Akaia asor akekroi atum ta ,nanglitum ason nang charlidunlo.

8*Nanglitum along pen Hemphu alam Masedonia lapen Akaia ahavar an ta lepetlo, la-an ta kalilang, Arnam aphan nanglitum akekroi alamjir ta bithe bitha chethangpetlo; lasi nclitum ke ajat ta pu jokphlotlo.

Thesalonkia asor angbong Pol akam :
1*Korte atum, nanglitum along nelitum vangnakne pu nanglitum mamat ta chinilo.

2*Ha Philippi nelitum kesung kelak pho-olo lapen bang atum ne patheraklo pu ta nanglitum chinilo; nanglitum along kevang ahut ta nelitum bang arideng kesung phonang-olo seta Armam jakong ne pi si nanglitum aphan Arnam aBirta Keme nang than longlo.

3*Nanglitum along nelitum Birta Keme nang kethan ke bang ne kapamanghu pen ta kali, kahingno kamatha pen ta kali lapen nang kachomosoiji aphan ta' kali.

4*Nelitum Birta Keme kapache thangji a-ot do pu Arnam ne chini si alangli si labangso akam ne senailo, lasi monit aning kapedokji aphan kali, ilitum aning kethekplot a-Arnam aning kapedokji aphan si nelitum kethan.

5*Nelitum lam nang kethan ke nanglitum aphan nang kemoi ta kali lapen tangka kelongji abidi le keri ta kali, lake keri ta kali, lake nanglitum chinilo lapen Arnam ta chinilo.

6*Nelitum ke Khrist aSangho atum pinak apot nanglitum along man peng chohang un, seta nanglitum along tame, bang along tame nelitum laheihei aman apeng ke ridamde.

7*Apei le asomar aphan kachinghonju pen kachelana asonthot nelitum ta nanglitum aphan' nang chinghon.

8*Nelitum nanglitum aphan nang chinghon-o apot Arnam aBirta Keme le nelitum nang kethan chot kali, bonta nanglitum ason thakthak nang kedodunji ta ning hang, pima nanglitum aphan nelitum nang chinghonpik. nuilenen.

9*Korte atum, nelitum Arnam aBirta Keme nanglitum angbong nang kapachethang ahut nanglitum aphan nang kapasiksaksedetji aphan nelitum jo arni ta kam keklem chepeduklo pu nanglitum ta ne chinilo.

10*Nanglitum kekroi atum angbong nelitum kedo kethak a-ot adur ingthirset, ahokpet lapen thangnat ta din move lake move lake nanglitum ta chinilo lapen Arnam ta chinilo.

11*Apo le asomar aphan kepere kapheleng asonthot nelitum ta nanglitum aphan nang pere nang phelenglo.

12*Arnam arat lapen adak atur angbong kedodun longji aphan nanglitum aphan nang kehang abang atheke Arnam aning kehang a-ot adur ateng nanglitum kedo thekji aphan nelitum ning nang peruniglo lapen nang pedotoilo.

Thesalonika asor akekroi lapen aning keding :
13*Nelitum Arnam aphan ning kachingdukver athe non eson dolang, atheke nanglitum angbong nelitum nang kethan Arnam alam ke monit alam kali, bonta Arnam alanm binong lo pu nanglitum kroilo lapen kalang ta kalang lo; pima labangso alam ke nanglitum kekroi atum angbong kedo Arnam ajakong lo.

14*Korte atum, ha Judia kedo Arnam amondoli asor, atheke Jisu Khrist arat atum kesung kepho asonthot si nanglitum ta pholo. Alanglitum le Jihudi atum arideng kechenek kaholo kepho nang asonthot nanglitum ta nanglitum adet asor arideng pho nanglo.

15*Jihudi atum si Jisu Khrist aphan lapen kethanmek atum aphan ta kapethi lapen nelitum aphan ta ne kephlung. Alanglitum kachomatha akam Arnam aphan ta aning pedokde lapen monit aphan ta aning pangsamse.

16*Honghari atum kejok-i aBirta Keme alanglitum angbong nelitum kapachethang longledetji aphan si Jihudi atum kabor-i. Lapuson si alanglitum apap thebomlo. Arnam aning kethi pen alanglitum kepho nangji akesung si alanglitum pholoklo.

18*Lasi nanglitum along nelitum vang ingtung-olo, neli Pol abatai batai ta bor-ilo, bonta hi-i neli phan ne khangthip.

19*Mo ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist kevangthuji ahut alangli ingno nelitum aning kethe, aning arong kalite kachipindak-i longji aphutup ke nanglitum helo kali ma? 20*Nelitum adak atur lapen aning arong ke nanglitum helo.

Kangthir lapen ning keme pen kedo :
4.
1*Korte atum, Arnam aphan ning kapedokji aphan nanglitum kedo kethak nangji aron nanglitum nelitum along pen nang charlidunlo. Nanglitum aphrang pen ta lapuson a-ot adur keme pen si dobomlo tema, bonta nanglitum a-ot adur an sose kachepemeji aphan nelitum Hemphu Jisu amen pen nang choningrilo.

2*Hemphu Jisu amen pen, nelitum nanglitum aphan nang kethan alam nanglitum chinilo

3*Armam aning kehang ke la helo: nanglitum chepangthirset nangji lapen mek langno no langno akam che-o nangji.

4*Nanglitum aning chekhangtu, nanglitum a-ok chepahijimri.

5*Ajat Kaprek atum Arnam aphan kachinine pen keklem ason ke aderi chepedori.

6*Badu tame a-ok avi kachepho akam pen ajok akorte aphan aning Pape-oi-enang lapen amahang Papajuremrenang, pima nelitum nang kapacheremek asonthot laheihei akam apot Hemphu si alar huipo.

7*Ilitum kachepahijim-maiji aphan le Arnam ekehang kali, bonta kachepangthirsetji aphan si ekehang.

8*La apot si labangso alam kapatepek abang aling ke monit aphan kali bonta Karjong Kangthir nang kepi abang atheke Arnam aphan si kapatepek lo.

9*Korte atum aphan kanghon alamjir badu ta nanglitum aphan kido nang tok ting-ei nangnelo, pima kachinghon seroi nangji alam Arnam nanglitum aphan nang thantanglo.

10*Masedonia kedo nanglitum akorte atum aphan nanglitum inghon ta inghonlo. Bonta nanglitum an sose chinghontha pu si nelitum nang choningrilo.

11*Nelitum nang kethan asonthot ning kangsam pen le donon, nanglitum methang methang akam le chemathanon lapen nanglitum ari hongkep pen le choklem chodamnon.

12*Anke nanglitum aphan bang nang rojopo lapen nanglitum bang aphan ta chiki jokphlotpo.
