Lam aphrang
Labangso akitap ketok abang ke Birta Keme pen apot bang ha Patmos pu alanglom along kephlugjui abang John helo pu si kachepu (l:14. 9: 22:8). Alangli methang le ketoiltang abang pu chepupe seta alangli ke Birta Keme phli abang ketok abang Jisu aketoitang abang John lo pu Mondoli alam kabirti aSarthe atum pulo. Bìrta Keme phli abang along ketok alam lapen Kapeklang Akitap along ketok alam lapen ketok aron, along along kacheton la do, kachetonte ta do. 81- 96 A.D. aningkan malon Domisian recho kethe Khristian atum aphan kachenek kaholo ahut si labangso akitap ketok.

Labangso akitap along ke halabangso ahut, non akai lapen mo aphi kephoji akesung aphan si alamlir ben ason si kejongdam. Bisar alam:s (a) Anu hong throksi lapen amek long throksi kedo aMeresak aso alam ke Jisu Khrist ke jakong horang kethe lapen kachinipet abang pu si kapeklang (5:6).

(b) Labangso akitap along ason ason kalakha aphai ta ketok do: 4 = Pirthe
6 = kechok-limle: kahingno
7 = kecchok-lim
12 =Israel akhei khei krehini; Sangho bang krehini
