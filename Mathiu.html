MATHIU KETOK ABIRTA KEME

Lam aphrang :
Mathiu Ketok aBirta Keme akitap ketok abang ke aphrang ha Kapharnaum medu kerung abang Levi pu abang Mathiu helo. Jisu alangli phan hang si aSangho bang krehini angbong inut pemanlo (Mt. 9:9-13; 10:3; Mk.2:13-17; Lk. 5:27-32; Akam. 1:13).

'Mathiu ketok aBirta Keme' pu akitap ke Mathiu si ketok. Alangli Palastain adet Jihudi atum angbong pen Khristian kemantang atum aphan si labangso aBirta Keme Aramaik alam pen si ketok pu 200 A.D. aningkan arjon angbong mondoli arlo ketok kelang atum an ta pupetlo. 50 A.D. aningkan malom Aramaik alam pen ketok halabangso aphrangsi akitap, helo bonta halabangso akitap ke virdetlo.

Non ilitum along kedo 'Mathiu ketok aBirta Keme' pu akitap ke 70-80 A.D. aningkan malom ketok akitap lo. Grik alam pen ketok labangso akitap Mathiu mamat si ketok ma kalite alangli long kedodun alangli sangho inut si ketok ma lake ilitum chinine, bonta aphrang akitap apar non kedo akitap chokmu lapen Aramaik alam pen Mathiu ketok aBirta Keme akitap lapen 50 A.D. aningkan malom ketok akaprek kaprek akitap atheke Mark ketok aBirta Kemne, Jisu kethan alam kapangrumtang akitap lapen Jesus long kedodun kethan alam heihui pen en si labangso akitap tok pamelo.

Akaprek atum ketok aBirta Keme apar Mathiu ketok aBirta Keme chokmu. Labangso akitap along Jisu kethan alam penang an kapangrum pen ketok do. Labangso akitap ke dhorom alam kethan-i akitap ason mandetlo. Aphrangsi aKhristian atum kardom kardi ahut labangso akitap si kaporhi. Jisu. kethan alam akenangsot atheke inglong athak kethan alam (Mt. 5,6,7) lapen akaprek kaprek Jisu alam an labangso akitap along ketok do.

Aban aron alam akimi aron angbong pechok-limlo pu Mathiu peklangdak-lo. Aban aron angbong ke 'Kangvaitang arat' ke Jihudi atum an chot, bonta akimi aron angbong ke pirthe adang amonit atum an aphan ta parloponlo. Pirthe adang amonit atum an aphan kapejokji aphan Jisu Khrist aban aron ahukum (Tora = kecheng, Kebar, Levi, Kalakha, Kethan) pechok-limlo.

Eklesia'(Mondoli) pu alam ketok abang ke Mathiu inut nat (Mt. 18:18; 18:17).: Monit aSopo arat ke (13:11: 16:28) ketheklongle arat chot kali, bonta Jisu kethan alamlir ateng ke theklong arat ta lo (Mt. 13:24-30, 36-40, 47-50). Labangso amondoli angbong rat atum do lapen rat atum aphan keja atum ta do (Mt. 16:1820; 18:15-20).

Mathiu ketok aBirta Keme ke inut aJihudi si Jihudi atumn aphan ketok alam lo. La apot si Jihudi atum angbong kethan atum (Rabi) apar Jisu themu (7:29); Jisu ke aron kapechok lim abang pinak apot aban aron ahukum an apar, hukum kepi abang Moses apar lapen Kethannek atum an apar ta Jisu themu (Mt. 5:21-22, 27-2g 21 34); Jisu ke Kesang arni ahemphu ta lo (Mt. 12:8) Kardom ahem thepi apar ta i themu (M. 12:6). Mathiu ke inut aJihudi lo lapen Jihudi atum aphan si laheihei alam ketok apot Kethanmek atum kethanmek alam an ta Jisu along si pachekok-lo atheke aphrang pen Arnam kechak "Kapejok abang" ke Jisu helo (Mt. 1:22; 2:15; 4:14; 27:9). 8:17; 13:35; 21:4; 26:56; *
