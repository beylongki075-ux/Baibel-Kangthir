Lam aphrang
Kristian arat atum angbong angthun kevangjir alam ateng Sebedi asopo John alangli kai kejangji dokdok malom ha Ephesus si alangli Birta Keme ketok. John ke Jakob akorte lo lapen sangho bang krehini atu angbong pen inut abang lo (Mt. 10:2; Lk. 6:14; Akam. 1:13). Alangli, alangli po penpen ha telong arlo kedo ahut si Jisu alangliphan kehang (M. 4:21; Mk. 1:19). Jisu alangliphan Sining kangder asopo' pusi ke-ir (Mk. 3:17). John ke Pitor lapen Jakob atum penpen Jisu kacheponver abang lo. Jisu alanglitum aphan alangli kam kenangsot an ahaki kapechodunji aphan si aning kehang: Alangli arje kachepharlo ahut (M. 17:1), Pitor anipi aphan kapeme ahut (Mk. 1:29), Jairus asopi aphan kaperengthu ahut (Mk. 5:37), Getsemani abiri arlo Arnam kardom ahut (Mt. 26:37). Jisu, Pitor lapen John aphan si kejok amei akechopi kapasikdamtoi (Lk. 22:8). Jisu, John lapen Jakob atum aphan ketam (Mt. 20:20-28). Jisu asangho kachinghonnei abang ke John helo. Alangli kaike ta Jisu along do, ha Kalvari anglong an ta alangli Jisu along dun (Jn. 13:23; 19:26-27; 20:2; 21:7,20-24). Pitor kardom ahem thepi angno kangni akeng katekok abang aphan kapeme ahut John ta alangli long do (Akam. 3:1), Jerusalem kangni ameipi along ta John do (Akam. 4:13, 19), ha Samaria kedam ahut ta John, Pitor along do (Akam.

8.:14), Pitor lapen Jakob penpen John ta Jerusalem amondoli asarthe atum angbong pen inut lo (Gal. 2:9).
