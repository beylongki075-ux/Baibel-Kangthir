PHILIPPI ASOR APHAN POL KETOK AKIDO

Lam aphrang
Philippi ke Masedonia ahavar isi anong athang kethe arongsopi lo. Pol batai hini aphong Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedam ahut si Philippi amondoli keseng50-51 A.D. aningkan (Akam. 16: 1040). Batai kethom aphong alangli Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedam ahut ta alangli ha Philippi dam (Akam 20:1).

Pol ha Rom phatek kedocheng ahut si-63 A.D. aningkan malom si labangso akido ketok. Philippi asor lapen Pol angbong kedo akachinghon seroi alam labangso akido pen klang-idak. Alangli phatek kedo ahut Philippi asor alangliphan kerapji pu tangka cherung si lodunlo lapen alangliphan kerapji aphan Epaphroditus aphan ta toidunlo. Pol alanglitum aphan ning chingduklo (Phil. 4:1).
