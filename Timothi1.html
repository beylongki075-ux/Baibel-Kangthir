TIMOTHI APHAN POL KETOK AKIDO HEIHEI

Lam aphrang
Timothi lapen Titus atum aphan Pol ketok akido heihei ke Mondoli aSarthe atum aphan ketok akido angse lo. Laheihei akido along ketok ke a-ovetin lapen alongritin mondoli keja atum aphan kapere kapheleng alam lo.

La pakthom akido ta alam chingbar-o. Birta. Keme kapachethang akam klem pemetu lapen klem chepeduktu pu si Pol, Timothi lapen Titus atum aphan perelo lapen kaphola alam pen ta pacheretoilo. Alanglitum aphan Khristian atum kedo kethak nangji aron, mondoli akam kapeklemji atum aphan kangvai lapen kebat nangji lapen kerung kerek nangji alam ta thanlo. Ahok aBirta Keme rim pemetu lapen otthiptu pu kachoningri pen si laheihei akido tok pejutlo.

Timothi :
Timothi ke Likonia ahavar Listra pu arong asor. Alangli po ke Grik lapen alangli pei ke Jihudi akhristianpi. 2 Timothi 1-5 along ketok ateng ke alangli pei amen ke Eunis pu lapen alangli phi amen ke Lois lapen Lois si Khristian keman paphrang. Birta Keme kapachethangji aphan batai hini aphong Pol kedam ahut Listra arong dongponput si Timothi aphan ari cheponlo (Akam. 16:1 4). Halabangso arni pen alangli Pol ajirpo manlo (1'Thes 3:2; 1 Kor. 4:17;16:10) lapen Pol aphan aphrang aphong ha Rom phatek kebeng (Kol. 1:1) arni anta alangli pen dam rap-raplo. Alangli ha Nihang anat puthot Pol along dunthulo seta alangliphan ke Ephesus amondoli aphan kapachariji aphan tekangbuplo. (4 Tim 1:3).

Timothi aphan Pol ketok aphrang akido ke 64-65 A.D. aningkan Masedonia pen si ketok. Kido hini abang ke 66-67 A.D. aningkan Pol kethiji dokdok ahut ha Rom arongsopi pen si ketok.
