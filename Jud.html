JUD KETOK AKIDO


Lam aphrang
Labangso akido ketok abang ke Jisu achakri' Jakob akorte Jud (Jud 1:) pu si kachepu. Jud ke Jisu asangho binong kali, bonta Jakob pen pum posar akorte pu si Baibel akuru atum kepu. 70-80 4.D. aningkan malom si nahok-hok kedo akhristian atum aphan si labangso akido ketok.
Labangso akido along athepi alam ke ahok-he alam kethan atum aphan kedin lapen kekroi kachepethir alam lo.

Angdeng kethak
1. Kecheng alam (1-2).
2. Ahok-he alam kethan atum (3-16)
3. Kekroi kachepethir (17-23).
4. Kerip alam (24-25).

Kardom kachepi :
1.
1*Lake Jisu Khrist aban lapen Jakob akorte Jud, Arnam kehangtang atum, Po Arnam kachinghon atum aphan ketok akido lo.

2*Kangjinso, ning kangsam lapen kanghon nanglitum along an sose pedonang.

Ahok-he alam kethan atum :
3*Kachinghonpar atum, ilitum kelong akejok alamjir nanglitum aphan nang ketokji neli ning hang. Arnam alangli kangthir atum aphan ephongvet kepidet akekroi kachepethirji aning nang kerungji aphan neli nanglitum aphan nang tokvek nangji pu si neli kamatha.

4*Nanglitum angbong kahingno amonit atum nang kacheju do. Halatum, ilitum a-Arnam ahirjume pen kapamekphrok-irong apot lapen ilitum aphan eKethan Abang lapen ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist aphan kekroi-kre apot alanglitum adai kedo ajir ding-olo.

5*Arnam alangli rat atum aphan ljip ahavar pen pejoklo, bonta aphi ke alangliphan kekroikre atum aphan ke alangli pevir-etlo pu alam nanglitum chinilo seta labangso alam nanglitum aphan ning nang kapamathathuji aphan si neli ning kehang.

6*Arnam apherangke atum a-ot adur ateng pen dodedet si amethang adim kache-o atum aphan Arnam ingchin ari pen ingnang-et si mo kangsai arni nang leletik an kangting aherangpi arlo jok-etlo pu alam ta mathadamtha.

7*Sodom lapen Gomora lapen lason thakthak adung arei arongsopi heihei asor a ot-e pen kangthirthe akam keklem apot kemep un-epin ame aherang jang-etlo, lake ilitum aphan ekapere-i lo.

8*Lason thakthak halabangso amonit atum ta amethang abang suva kacheperoj1, Arnam ajakong kekoiji lapen pherangke atum aphan kapinremji athe pedolo.

9*Pherangke kethe Maikel mamat ta Moses abang arpum hi-i pen kachethi ahut, alangli alang phan dindedet si ingtontedetlo bonta 'Hemphu le nang petam lonang' pu si puchot.

10*Bonta halabangso amonit atum ke kachinine aling si ington varet lapen kamatha thekthe api atum kachinidam ason alanglitum kachinidam aling si chepevirdet.

11*Halatum nahokdet pen ong. Pima halatum ke Kain asonthot si cheplangdetlo lapen amethaDg ale kachepedoji aphan Balam kethan alam kroidundetlo lapen Kora ason kachesong pen si chepevirdetlo.

12*Halatum amethang apok aphan chemathalok si therak-repin pen nanglitum along nang kechodun pen ajok nanglitum kecho rap-rap akam aman ave idetlo. Halatum ke tomon kevan arve kave anghun ason lo. alanglitum ke abotor ahut ta athe kapathethe kekrenghak lapen kepurtang athengpi ason lo.

13*Halatum ke talo alang apotani ason kachetherak pen angpip sucheilo lapen alanglitum ke kevirdam Arnam amethengtom ason lo, alanglitum aphan si kangting aherangpi kejut un-e akai aphan raipidetlo.

14*Lapuson amonit atum aphan matha si Adam pen ove throksi nang kechok a-ove amonit Enok pu abang thanmek si pulo: Ne nang pulang, suri suri amonit kangthir atum penpen Hemphu vangpo.

15*Monit aso an aphan ingsaipo lapen kahingno keklem atum aphan kahingno keklem an an apot lapen dhorom kave apapi atum alangliphan keputot kepusit apot alanglitum aphan pephosetpo."

16*Halatum ke kaningje pasilurtur-tar atum lo, kar-ithap ason ke-ong-ong apot amek kepleng un-e atum lo, kacheroka atum lo lapen amethang ale kelongdunji angring do lote bang aphan kero karcho atum lo. aphan kero karcho ta kaseletle-o-o atum lo.

Kerip alam
