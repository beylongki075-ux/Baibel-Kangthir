GALASIA ASOR APHAN POL KETOK AKIDO

Lam aphrang
Galasia asor aphan Pol ketok akido ke Pol batai kethom aphong Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedam ahut 54 - 56 A.D. aningkan angbong ha Ephesus pen si ketok. Galasia amondoli keseng ke Pol batai hini aphong Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedan ahut lo (Akam 16:6). Puthot batai kethom aphong Birta Keme kapachethangji pu kedam ahut alangli ha Galasia ta damlo (Akam.18:23)

Labangso akido pen Pol, Galasia amondoli angbong Jihudi pen Khristian kedun atum kepu alam atheke Pol ke sangho binong kali, lapen Khristian atum ta Jihudi aron heihei kroi nangji pu kepu atum aphan pedokliplo. Alangli pulo: "Monit le alangliphan sangho kebat kali, Arnam si kebat. Jihudi aron kekroi pen le monit atum aphan ahok abang pu kalakhaponji kali, Jisu Khrist aphan kekroi pen helo. Khrist aphan kekroi pen ajok si Khristian atum a-ot adur keme pen do thek thak thek pu klangdak."

Angdeng kethak
1.Kardom kackepi lapen ketam ---1:1-10
2.Birta Keme kapachethangji aphan Pol jakong kelong,alangli kethan alam an Jerusalem kedo asangho atum ameipi ta kekroidun ---1:11-2:21.
3.Aron pen le kejok kali,bonta Jisu Khrist pen si kejok,Baibel alam ke-en pen kethan --3:1-5:12.
4.Khristian a-ot adur pen nangji alam --5:13-6:18.

Kerip alam :
11*Neli ri methang pen neli nanglitum aphan amek kethe pen nang ketok alam langtha.

12*Amethang ale kerilok atum si nanglitum aphan ok kangduk areng angchun nang kapacherot bor-i. Khrist athengparpan pen ajok kachenek kaholo kepho jokphlot nangji aphan si alanglitum labangso akam keklem.

13*A-ok kangduk areng angchun kacherottang atum seta aron ateng pen dode, bonta aron petanglo pu kachepindak longji aphan si alanglitum nanglitum aphan a-ok kangduk areng angchun nang kapacherot chot.

14*Bonta neli ke ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist athengparpan apar ason kaprek alam pen apot ke neli chepindak-i-e. Alangli thengparpan pen apot pirthe ke neliphan lapen neli ta pirthe aphan thengparpan athak kethitang ason lo.

15*Pima a-ok kangduk areng angchun cherot seta cherot re seta ale ave; kethipmi abang kacheplang helo kenangsot.

16*Labangso aron ateng kedo atum aphuthak ning kangsam lapen kangjinso pedonang, alanglitum helo Arnam arat Israel binong.

17*Non pen apara ke badu ta neliphan ne papesungsenang, pima neli bang athak Jisu angthek do.

18*Korte atum, ilitum aHemphu Jisu Khrist ahirjume nanglitum akarjong along pedonang.Amen.
